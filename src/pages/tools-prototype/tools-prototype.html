Prototype

WHY

To build a minimum viable product that can be rapidly tested

WHAT

  • Building up a real, tactile/visual representation of your idea to deliver a realistic display of the prototype

HOW

  • Defining minimum features of the solution, getting concrete and visualizing it

What should a minimum viable solution look like?

  • What are the minimum features that are needed?
  • How can we make it simpler, easier, cheaper?
  • How can we visualize it?
