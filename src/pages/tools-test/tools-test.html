Prototype

WHY

Gaining customer feedback as quick as possible in order to decide whether to keep the solution, change it or stop working on it.

WHAT

  • Create a small experiment of how to test the solution and think about hypotheses, measurement of data, test design etc.

HOW

  • Design a cheap and simple experiment that allows to test the idea as quickly as possible and get feedback
  • Complete the test card (if running out of time, ensure there are at least Next Steps and Owner completed)

How/where/when/who will test the prototype?

Test Card
hypotheses What are our assumptions?
Test Design How will we conduct our experiment>
Metrics What is the one metric that matters?
Criteria What is needed to pass the test?
Investment How much money do we need for testing?
Next Steps What do we need prior and post-test?
Timeline When will we test the prototype?
Owner Who is responsible for following up?
