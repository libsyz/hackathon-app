EMPATHISE

WHY?

Making sure that our solution to the problem addresses the real needs and requirements of our user group.

WHAT?

Identifying needs, requirements & concerns for different groups of people that will be using/be affected by the solution.

HOW

Defining our user groups, drawing an adoption curve for each of them and empathising about the needs, requirements, potential contraints and concerns of early adopters, average users and laggards.

KEY QUESTIONS

  • Who are your users? Define up to three user/stakeholder groups
  • If you put yourself in the shoes of the user, what are their pain points, needs motivations, feelings?
  • What do the average users, early adopters and laggards care about?
